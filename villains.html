👹 Makuta — The Shadows of Mata Nui 👹

Makuta Teridax is the primary antagonist of the Bionicle saga — a being of pure shadow who betrayed his duty and sought to plunge Mata Nui into eternal darkness. He infected Rahi with his influence, created the Rahkshi from Kraata, and ruled from his lair beneath the island.

Makuta

Makuta Teridax

🌑 Shadow 🌑
Kanohi: Kraahkan (Mask of Shadows)
Lair: Mangaia (beneath the island of Mata Nui)
Personality: Cunning, patient, and utterly ruthless.
Powers: Shadow manipulation, shapeshifting, Rahi control, Kraata creation.
