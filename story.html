🌍 The Complete Legend of Bionicle 🌍

The full story — from the Great Spirit's slumber to the awakening of the Toa, the betrayal of Makuta, the cleansing of the Bohrok, and the birth of the seventh hero. This is the complete lore of Generation 1 (2001-2003).

🏙️ Part 1: Metru Nui — The City of Legends

Before the island of Mata Nui, before the Toa Mata, there was Metru Nui — a magnificent city inside the Great Spirit's head. This is where the story truly begins.

🏛️

The City of Metru Nui

Metru Nui was a vast, domed city located inside the head of the Great Spirit Mata Nui. It was divided into six districts, each corresponding to an element: Ta-Metru (Fire), Ga-Metru (Water), Ko-Metru (Ice), Onu-Metru (Earth), Po-Metru (Stone), and Le-Metru (Air). The Matoran worked in factories, forging Kanoka disks and Kanohi masks.

The city was powered by the Great Furnace and connected by a network of chutes and railways. The Matoran lived peacefully for thousands of years under the protection of the Toa Metru — until Makuta's betrayal.

👑

The Toa Metru

The Toa Metru were six heroes chosen to protect Metru Nui: Vakama (Fire), Nokama (Water), Nuju (Ice), Whenua (Earth), Onewa (Stone), and Matau (Air). They were created from Matoran by Toa power and were the predecessors of the Turaga who would later lead the villages on Mata Nui.

Each Toa Metru wielded unique weapons: Vakama used a disk launcher, Nokama used hydro blades, Nuju used crystal spikes, Whenua used drill claws, Onewa used a barbed staff, and Matau used aero slicers.

⚡ The Great Cataclysm

Makuta Teridax, the guardian of Metru Nui, betrayed his duty. He cast a spell of darkness over the Great Spirit, plunging Mata Nui into a deep sleep. The city of Metru Nui was thrown into chaos. The Toa Metru, newly created, had to evacuate all Matoran from the city before it collapsed. They led the Matoran through a dangerous underground labyrinth to the surface — the island of Mata Nui. When they arrived, they sacrificed their Toa power to awaken the Matoran, becoming the Turaga elders. The Matoran had no memory of Metru Nui, and the Toa Metru became legend.

The Great Cataclysm also caused earthquakes across the universe. The Matoran of Metru Nui were placed in Matoran spheres for the journey, which erased their memories. The Toa Metru's sacrifice is remembered as one of the greatest acts of heroism in Bionicle history.

🏝️ Part 2: The Island of Mata Nui

For 1,000 years, the Matoran lived on the island of Mata Nui — the face of the sleeping Great Spirit. They built six villages, unaware of the darkness growing beneath their feet.

🔥

Ta-Koro

Village of Fire, built on a volcanic crater. Led by Turaga Vakama. The Ta-Matoran were brave warriors and skilled guards.

💧

Ga-Koro

Village of Water, built on floating lily pads. Led by Turaga Nokama. The Ga-Matoran were expert sailors and fishers.

❄️

Ko-Koro

Village of Ice, perched on Mount Ihu. Led by Turaga Nuju. The Ko-Matoran were wise and contemplative.

⛰️

Onu-Koro

Village of Earth, hidden in underground caverns. Led by Turaga Whenua. The Onu-Matoran were miners and inventors.

🪨

Po-Koro

Village of Stone, carved into desert canyons. Led by Turaga Onewa. The Po-Matoran were sculptors and builders.

🌪️

Le-Koro

Village of Air, built in jungle treetops. Led by Turaga Matau. The Le-Matoran were musicians and Gukko riders.

⚔️ Part 3: The Toa Mata — Heroes Arrive

For 1,000 years, the Matoran prayed for heroes. Their prayers were answered when six canisters washed ashore.

📦

The Canisters

Six canisters emerged from the sea, each carrying a Toa Mata. They had no memory of their past — only a mission. Tahu landed on Ta-Wahi beach. Gali arrived at Naho Bay. Kopaka washed up on the shores of Ko-Wahi. Onua emerged in Onu-Wahi. Pohatu landed in Po-Wahi. Lewa crashed into the jungles of Le-Wahi. They met the Turaga, who explained their destiny: find their Great Masks, unite the Matoran, and defeat Makuta.

🎭

The Quest for Masks

Each Toa had to find six Great Kanohi masks hidden in shrines across the island. They journeyed through dangerous territories, fighting infected Rahi — creatures corrupted by Makuta's influence. Takua, the wandering Chronicler, helped document their quest. When all six masks were found, the Toa Mata gained their full power and confronted Makuta in his lair beneath the island.

The final battle against Makuta took place in Mangaia, his underground lair. The Toa fought shadows and illusions, eventually striking Makuta with all six elements combined. Though they didn't destroy him, they weakened him enough to end his reign of terror — for now.

🕹️ Part 4: The Mata Nui Online Game (MNOG)

The Mata Nui Online Game was the definitive way to experience the story. It followed Takua, the Chronicler, as he explored the island and witnessed the Toa's journey.

📜

Takua's Journey

Players controlled Takua as he traveled from village to village, helping Matoran, solving puzzles, and uncovering the secrets of Mata Nui. He found the Toa stones, witnessed the arrival of the Toa Mata, and helped them locate their Great Masks. The game ended with Takua and the Toa entering Makuta's lair, where Takua recited the legend of the Toa while they fought the Manas crabs.

🎵

The Music of MNOG

Composed by Justin Luchter, the game's soundtrack became iconic. "Ta-Koro" captured the heat and danger of the Fire Village. "Le-Koro" felt like a jungle celebration. "The Final Battle" was tense and epic. For many fans, the music of MNOG is the sound of Bionicle.

The soundtrack was never officially released, but fans have preserved it online. Many still listen to it while reading Bionicle comics or building sets.

🪲 Part 5: The Bohrok — Cleaners of the World

After Makuta's defeat, a new threat emerged — not evil, but unstoppable. The Bohrok swarms rose from their underground hives to "cleanse" the island.

🥚

The Hives and Krana

Beneath Mata Nui, thousands of Bohrok slept in organic hives. Each Bohrok was controlled by a Krana — a living, intelligent slug-like creature that piloted the mechanical shell. The Bohrok's purpose was to wipe the island clean of all life and structures, returning it to a blank slate.

⚡

The Toa Nuva Transformation

The Toa Mata were no match for the Bohrok swarms. They immersed themselves in Energized Protodermis — a mysterious, living substance that transforms whatever it touches. They emerged as the Toa Nuva, with enhanced powers, Nuva Masks, and new weapons. Only then could they defeat the Bahrag queens — Cahdok and Gahdok — and seal them in a cage of protodermis, ending the swarm.

⚙️ Part 6: The Bohrok-Kal — Enhanced Warriors

After the Bahrag were sealed, six silver-armored Bohrok-Kal emerged. They were smarter, stronger, and more dangerous than their predecessors.

🔘

The Nuva Symbols

The Bohrok-Kal were sent to retrieve the Nuva Symbols — eight medallions linked to the Toa Nuva's power. Each Kal had a unique elemental shadow power: Fire Shadow, Water Shadow, Ice Shadow, Stone Shadow, Earth Shadow, and Air Shadow. They could think and strategize, unlike regular Bohrok.

💥

The Defeat of the Kal

The Toa Nuva fought the Bohrok-Kal across the island. In the final battle, the Kal overloaded their powers trying to break the cage of the Bahrag. They were destroyed, and the Nuva Symbols were returned. The Bohrok threat was finally over.

✨ Part 7: The Mask of Light — The Seventh Toa

In 2003, a new legend began — the search for the Mask of Light and the Toa who would wear it.

💡

The Avohkii

The Mask of Light, known as the Avohkii, was discovered by Takua and Jaller in the ruins of a shrine. A mysterious voice told them to carry the mask to the seven temples and find the seventh Toa — the Toa of Light. They journeyed across Mata Nui, pursued by Rahkshi — terrifying creatures spawned from Makuta's Kraata.

⚔️

The Birth of Takanuva

After Jaller was struck down protecting him, Takua placed the Mask of Light on his own face. He was transformed into Takanuva — the Toa of Light, riding a Ussal crab named Pewku. He entered Makuta's lair, defeated Teridax in a final battle, and brought light back to Mata Nui. Jaller was revived, and the Great Spirit began to stir.

🌅 Part 8: The Final Battle and Awakening

The legend of Mata Nui reached its climax as the Toa Nuva and Takanuva prepared for the end.

🌌

The Great Spirit Awakens

With Makuta defeated, the Great Spirit Mata Nui began to rise from his slumber. The island of Mata Nui crumbled and broke apart, revealing the giant robot beneath. The Matoran, Toa, and Turaga escaped to a new homeland, leaving the old island behind.

📖

The Legacy

The Toa Nuva and Takanuva continued to protect the Matoran in their new home. Makuta was not truly gone — his spirit lived on, waiting for another chance. But for now, Mata Nui was at peace. The legend of Bionicle would continue for years to come, but the story of the Toa Mata — the heroes who washed ashore on a mysterious island — remains the heart of it all.
